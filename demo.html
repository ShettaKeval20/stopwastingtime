GOAL LADDER · STEP 01 / 05
What is your big goal?
One outcome by December 2028. Specific and measurable — not a feeling, a result.
Weak: “Get fit” · “Make more money” · “Be successful”
Strong: “RECE designation + permanent daycare supervisor role” · “$50K TFSA portfolio” · “YouTube channel at 100K subs”
GOAL LADDER · STEP 02 / 05
Reality check
A goal that fails any of these will quietly die by March. Check each one honestly.
It is measurable — I will know with certainty when it is done
It depends on MY actions — not on someone else’s decision
It has a hard deadline — December 2028, no extensions
I can name the daily habits that lead to it
GOAL LADDER · STEP 03 / 05
The 90-day question
If December 2028 is the destination — what must be TRUE in 90 days for this goal to stay alive? Not finished. Alive.
Example: Big goal “RECE + supervisor role” → 90-day: “CECE equivalency submitted + working full-time in an ECE role”
GOAL LADDER · STEP 04 / 05
This month’s output
What single thing gets SHIPPED this month that proves progress? An output you can point to — not effort, not hours.
Outputs: “Application submitted” · “10 videos published” · “Certification exam booked” — things that exist after you finish.
