Questions
Does it require an internet connection?
No. The app is a single HTML file that runs entirely in your browser. Your data is saved locally. No account, no server, no tracking.
What browser does it work in?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — any modern browser on desktop or mobile.
Is this a subscription?
No. One-time payment. You own it forever. Updates are free for Standard and Premium tiers.
Can I lose my data?
Your data lives in your browser’s localStorage. The app has a built-in JSON export/import feature for backups. Export regularly.

Those are trackers. This is a system. It has cue-routine-reward mapping, identity-based goal ladders, AI coaching, path fork decisions for bad habits, streak shields, and 30+ more features specifically designed around behaviour change science — not just checkboxes.
